5 Nonclinical Pharmacology/Toxicology
Prior approvals for DTG and RPV included comprehensive nonclinical packages. In agreement
with ICH Guidance M3 (R2), nonclinical combination studies were not
conducted with DTG/RPV, except for a combination pharmacokinetic study in dogs, to
determine any potential effects of coͲadministration.

(15EDS002 or 2015N239161)

Reviewer comments
This is a bioequivalence study to bridge the clinical formulations (single entity products used in
Phase 3 trials) to the toͲbeͲmarketed formulation (JULUCA FDC). The sponsor conducted a fed
BE study since Phase 3 trials (SWORDͲ1/2) were conducted under fed conditions due to
rilpivirine (to be administered under fed conditions only).
Inclusion and exclusion criteria
Healthy male or female subjects between 18 and 55 years of age (inclusive) with a body weight
> 50 kg (110 lbs) for men and >45 kg (99 lbs) for women and body mass index (BMI) within the
range 18.5 to 31 kg/m2(inclusive) were eligible to enter the study. Subjects with history or
presence of any illness or conditions that might confound the results of the study or poses an
additional risk to the subject were excluded.
Prohibited medications
Subjects abstained from taking any other prescription and nonͲprescription drugs within 7 days
(or 14 days if the drug is an inducer) or 5 halfͲlives (whichever was longer) prior to the first dose
of study medication until completion of the followͲup visit (with the exception of <2 grams of
acetaminophen any time outside of the first 10 hours postͲdose). Use of H2Ͳblockers, protonͲ
pump inhibitors, antacids, vitamins, calcium or iron supplements were strictly prohibited within
7 days prior to the first dose of study medication and for the duration of the trial, including
followͲup.
Pharmacokinetic assessments
Pharmacokinetic samples were collected preͲdose and up to 264 hours post dose. Standard PK
parameters were determined.
Bioanalysis
The plasma concentrations of DTG and RPV were determined by validated analytical methods.
The standard curve and QC data indicate that assays were precise and accurate. All samples
were stored and processed in the time frame supported by the stability data. The ZͲisomer can
be formed from RPV by ambient light. Therefore, the analyses were conducted under yellow
light and the amount of zͲisomer was monitored.
